About the Project

Food Ordering Behaviour and Consumer Trends is a Tableau project that analyzes customer food ordering patterns and purchasing behaviour.

It helps businesses understand customer preferences, ordering trends, and sales performance through interactive dashboards and visualizations.

Project Objectives

🍽️

Analyze Ordering Behaviour

Study customer ordering patterns across different food categories, meal types, and preferences to identify popular choices.

💲

Analyze Pricing Impact

Evaluate how food prices, delivery charges, and discounts influence customer purchasing decisions and order value.

📍

Optimize Restaurant Performance

Analyze restaurant categories, locations, and customer demand to improve visibility and increase sales.

👥

Customer Analysis

Understand customer demographics, repeat orders, and purchasing behaviour to identify consumer trends.

🎯

Promotion Effectiveness

Measure the impact of discounts and promotional campaigns on order frequency, sales volume, and customer engagement.

📅

Seasonal Ordering Trends

Analyze ordering patterns based on seasons, weather conditions, and time periods to support better business decisions.
